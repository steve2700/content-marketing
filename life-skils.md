## 🌞 Bonus: The Power of the Sun

> _"And We have made the sun a lamp providing light."_  
> — **Qur'an, Surah Nuh (71:16)**

- Improves sleep, focus, testosterone, energy.
- Increases gratitude and connection to nature (Fitrah).
